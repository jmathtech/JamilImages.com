About



Jamil Matheny is a multi-talented individual with a passion for technology and creative expression.

As a developer, he has a wealth of experience in photography, various programming languages and technologies, including web development, mobile app development, and data analysis.

He shares his knowledge and insights through his YouTube channel, where he creates videos on various tech-related topics, ranging from tutorials on specific programming languages to discussions on the latest trends in the industry.

Aside from his work in technology, Jamil is also an accomplished photographer who enjoys capturing beautiful moments and stunning landscapes.

He often incorporates his love for photography into his tech-related content, showing viewers how to edit photos using various software tools.

As a learner, Jamil is constantly seeking new challenges and experiences. He has a deep curiosity about the world and a drive to learn and grow as a person and professional.

If you're interested in following his work, you can check out his website, jamilimages.com, and his YouTube channel at youtube.com/@jmathtech.

Jamil Images, LLC was established by Jamil Matheny in March 2019.

Jamil's passion for photography began decades ago and has since expanded to include a wide range of events and subjects.

With a focus on capturing the essence of concerts, community events, businesses, and products, Jamil has established a dynamic portfolio of stunning images.

FAQ

Q: What is Jamil Images?
A: Jamil Images, LLC. is a photography services company that provides high-quality photography services for a variety of events, studio portraits and photo prints. Our clients schedule an appointment and the photographer will be present for that appointment time. After the appointment, your photos will be edited and uploaded to the client gallery with a password to access them. Photo prints are sold separately in the client's gallery. Schedule an appointment today and watch us work our magic!

Q: How long does it take to ship print orders from Jamil Images?
A: Shipping usually takes between 3-5 business days. However, delivery times may vary depending on your location and the availability of the product you ordered.

Q: Do you have a studio?
A: Yes! Jamil Images is located inside the Supreme Flea Market at 3302 Williamsburg Rd. Richmond, VA. Ste #42.

Q: Can I book a Jamil Images photographer for a last-minute event?
A: It's best to book a photographer in advance to ensure their availability, but Jamil Images may be able to accommodate last-minute bookings depending on their schedule.

Q: What is the cost of Jamil Images photography services?
A: The cost of Jamil Images photography services may vary depending on the type of event and the amount of coverage needed. Book an appointment with us today or contact Jamil Images for a quote based on your specific needs.

Q: Can I order prints of the photos taken by Jamil Images?
A: Yes, Jamil Images offers a range of print options, including canvas prints, framed prints, and photo albums. Contact Jamil Images for more information about print options and pricing.

Q: Can I see examples of Jamil Images' previous work?
A: Yes, Jamil Images has a portfolio of their previous work on our website. Just click on the client gallery.

Q: What if I have specific requests or ideas for my event's photography?
A: Jamil Images is open to hearing your specific requests and ideas and will work with you to create a customized package that fits your needs. Contact us to discuss your ideas and preferences.
